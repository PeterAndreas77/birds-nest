How To Use


Flight Plans


Plan the place and dates before you travel!

To create a plan, click hatch a plan on the top left, then fill the form.

To update a plan, click update for the plan you want to update.

To delete a plan, click delete for the plan you want to delete.


Flight Logs


Log your story and rate it after your travel!

To create a log, first click flight logs on the side menu bar. Then you will be taken to the flight logs screen. If you have not created any log, it will be empty. Once you are in the flight logs screen, click pick a plan on the top left. Your flight plans will be generated. Pick a plan and click log to record your story and rating for that plan.

To update a log, click update for the log you want to update.

To delete a log, click delete for the log you want to update.
